## My own projects

- [Tech.Interviewer](https://techinterview.space?source=mgorbatyuk.dev) - a platform for collecting and analyzing IT salaries in Kazakhstan. Helps people to understand the market and make better salary decisions. In 2025, a section for company reviews was added.
- [EV Charge Tracker](https://mgorbatyuk.dev/ev-charging-tracker/?source=mgorbatyuk.dev) - iOS app for tracking EV (Electric Vehicle) charging and other type of expenses costs. Built with SwiftUI and Swift using Claude.
- [Individual Entrepreneur in Kazakhstan](https://maximgorbatyuk.notion.site/ip-kazakhstan-tutorial?source=mgorbatyuk.dev?source=mgorbatyuk.dev) - a knowledge base about Individual Entrepreneur (ru: "ИП") in Kazakhstan. It helps people to understand the process of registration and running IE.
